### English Versions (Updated with Language Switcher)

## How It Works

### For New Recipes:
1. **Send me the recipe link**
2. **I extract the ingredients & instructions**
3. **I categorize it** (Platos Principales / Postres / Bebidas, etc.)
4. **I translate to Spanish** (full translation + cultural adaptation)
5. **I create both versions:**
   - English: `recipe-name.html`
   - Spanish: `recipe-name-es.html`
6. **I add language switchers** to both versions
7. **I update both homepages** (index.html & index-es.html)
8. **You upload the files** to your hosting

### Translation Quality Standards:
- Professional Spanish translation (not machine-only)
- Cultural food terminology adaptation (e.g., "pimiento morrón" for bell pepper in Mexico)
- Cooking method clarity in Spanish
- Same formatting & styling in both languages
